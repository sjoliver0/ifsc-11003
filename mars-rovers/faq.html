Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long can a rover operate on Mars?
A: Solar-powered rovers (Spirit & Opportunity) were designed for ~90 sols but lasted much longer. Curiosity and Perseverance use MMRTG nuclear power for multi-year missions.
Q: Can rovers drill into rocks?
A: Yes. Curiosity and Perseverance both drill and analyze rock samples. Perseverance stores sample tubes for future return to Earth.
Q: What technology was first demonstrated by Perseverance?
A: The Ingenuity helicopter made the first powered flights on another planet, and the MOXIE experiment produced oxygen from CO₂.
Q: Where can I find official NASA images?
A: Visit NASA's public-domain image archive at images.nasa.gov.
